# Google Play Console — Step by Step Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create the App
1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Click **"Create app"**
3. Fill in:
   - **App name:** Brain Box: Strategy Games
   - **Default language:** English (United States)
   - **App or game:** Game
   - **Free or paid:** Free
4. Accept the declarations and click **Create app**

## Step 2: Store Listing (Main store listing)
1. Go to **Grow > Store presence > Main store listing**
2. Fill in the **Short description** and **Full description** from above
3. **App icon:** Upload the 512×512 icon (from `assets/icon.png`)
4. **Feature graphic:** Create a 1024×500 banner image with "Brain Box" title and game icons
5. **Screenshots:** Upload at least 2 phone screenshots (1080×1920 or similar)
   - Take screenshots from the preview APK on your phone
   - Recommended: Home screen, a game in progress, daily challenge, settings
6. Click **Save**

## Step 3: Content Rating
1. Go to **Policy > App content > Content rating**
2. Click **Start questionnaire**
3. Select **"Utility, Productivity, Communication, or Other"** → then **"Game"**
4. Answer NO to all questions about violence, sexual content, drugs, gambling, etc.
5. You'll receive an **"Everyone"** rating
6. Click **Apply**

## Step 4: Target Audience
1. Go to **Policy > App content > Target audience**
2. Select age groups: **All ages** (or "13 and above" to keep it simple)
3. If you select children under 13, you'll need to comply with additional policies
4. **Recommended:** Select "13+" to avoid extra requirements

## Step 5: Data Safety
1. Go to **Policy > App content > Data safety**
2. Answer the questionnaire:
   - **Does your app collect or share data?** → Yes (ads collect device identifiers)
   - **Data types collected:** Device identifiers (for ads)
   - **Is data encrypted in transit?** → Yes
   - **Can users request data deletion?** → N/A (no personal data stored on servers)
3. If you haven't integrated real ads yet, you can select **No data collected**

## Step 6: App Access
1. Go to **Policy > App content > App access**
2. Select **"All functionality is available without special access"**

## Step 7: Ads Declaration
1. Go to **Policy > App content > Ads**
2. Select **"Yes, my app contains ads"** (if you've integrated AdMob)
3. Or **"No"** if ads aren't live yet

## Step 8: Privacy Policy
1. Go to **Policy > App content > Privacy policy**
2. Enter URL: `https://spicearcstudio-cmd.github.io/BrainBox/`

## Step 9: Create a Release
1. Go to **Release > Production**
2. Click **"Create new release"**
3. Upload the `.aab` file
4. **Release name:** 2.0.0 (auto-filled)
5. **Release notes:**
```
🎉 Welcome to Brain Box!

5 classic strategy games in one app:
• Dots & Boxes, Tic Tac Toe, Connect Four, Memory Match, Color Flood
• Play vs AI or 2 Players on the same device
• Daily Challenges with streak tracking
• 4 beautiful color themes
• Game stats and win tracking
• Parental controls for screen time management
• Sound effects and celebration animations
```
6. Click **Review release** → **Start rollout to Production**

## Step 10: Wait for Review
- First review typically takes **1–3 business days**
- You'll get an email when the app is approved or if changes are needed
