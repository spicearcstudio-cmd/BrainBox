## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Brain Box brings 5 classic strategy and puzzle games to your phone in one beautiful, cheerful app!

GAMES INCLUDED:

- DOTS & BOXES - Connect the dots, claim the boxes, outsmart the AI
- TIC TAC TOE - The classic X and O game with bigger grids
- CONNECT FOUR - Drop discs and connect four in a row
- MEMORY MATCH - Flip cards and find all matching pairs
- COLOR FLOOD - Flood the board with one color in limited moves

FEATURES:
- 5 classic games in one lightweight app
- Multiple difficulty levels with scaling grid sizes
- Smart AI opponents that adapt to your skill level
- Beautiful cheerful design with 4 color themes
- Haptic feedback for immersive gameplay
- Share your scores and challenge friends
- Works offline with Premium

DIFFICULTY SCALING:
Each game gets harder with bigger boards:
- Dots & Boxes: 4x4 to 7x7 grids
- Tic Tac Toe: 3x3 to 5x5 boards
- Connect Four: Standard and large boards
- Memory Match: 6 to 10 pairs
- Color Flood: 8x8 to 14x14 grids

PREMIUM ($2.99 one-time):

- Unlock exclusive color themes (Ocean, Sunset, Forest)

Perfect for quick games on the go. Challenge yourself against increasingly difficult AI opponents!

## Steps to Publish

1. Register at https://play.google.com/console ($25 one-time fee)
2. Run `npx eas build --platform android --profile production`
3. Download the .aab file from EAS
4. Create new app in Play Console
5. Upload the .aab to Production track
6. Fill in store listing (above content)
7. Upload screenshots (min 4 required)
8. Complete content rating questionnaire (select "Everyone")
9. Add privacy policy URL
10. Set pricing to "Free" with in-app purchases
11. Submit for review (typically 1-7 days)
