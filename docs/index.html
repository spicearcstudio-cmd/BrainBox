Privacy Policy

Brain Box — Classic Strategy Games

Last updated: June 30, 2026

SpiceArc Studio ("we", "us", or "our") built Brain Box as a freemium mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Brain Box is designed to operate primarily offline. We collect minimal data to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Game preferences & progress — stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This includes your selected theme, game statistics, daily challenge progress, and parental control settings. This data never leaves your device.
  • Purchase information — if you choose to make an in-app purchase (premium upgrade), the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Advertising

The free version of Brain Box may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These services may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Premium users do not see any advertisements.

You can review Google AdMob's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Brain Box includes a parental control feature that allows parents or guardians to limit the number of games played per day. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or login.

Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store user data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Third-Party Services

Brain Box may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for in-app purchases)
  • Google AdMob (for advertisements in the free version)
  • Expo Application Services (for app updates)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
